Welcome to the Book Club

Love reading books?

Then you are in the right place! Welcome to my book club. My name is Kristina and as you can guess I love reading books. I decided to open this book club for meeting new people all around the world, who shares same interest as me. Its a place where you can discuss your favorite books, find new recommedations and even meet offline. Every month you can find a book in any genre you are interested in, read it and then come back to a blog where you can discuss it with others.
